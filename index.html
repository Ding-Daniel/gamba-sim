Blackjack setup
Choose which seat you control. Your bankroll and bet apply to the seat you control.
Rules: dealer stands on soft 17. Actions: hit/stand, double (one card then stand), split (same-rank pair; one split). Betting: win 1:1, blackjack 3:2, push returns, loss loses bet.
Borrowing warning
You borrowed money to cover a bet. Borrowing is automatic once your bet goes past your available bankroll.
  • Each single borrow is capped at $500,000.
  • If you still have debt after every 2 completed rounds, the debt compounds by 10%.
  • Winnings automatically reduce debt.
  • Reset clears everything and restores the bankroll to $100,000.
